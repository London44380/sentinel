# Contributing to Sentinel

Thank you for your interest in Sentinel.

However, this project is **not open for external contributions**.

- Pull requests will generally be closed without review.
- Feature requests and suggestions may be ignored or declined.
- Forking and derivative works are **not permitted** under the proprietary license.

If you discover a critical bug or security issue, you may contact the author
privately or open a minimal issue describing the problem **without** sharing
sensitive details or exploit code.
